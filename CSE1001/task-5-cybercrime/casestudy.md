## Phishing Case Study

Phishing is a cybercrime where attackers trick users into revealing sensitive information such as passwords or bank details.

In a typical case, a student receives an email pretending to be from a bank. The email contains a link asking the user to "verify" their account. Once clicked, the user enters their details on a fake website.

Students and young users are often targeted due to lack of awareness. The consequences include financial loss and identity theft.

This highlights the importance of verifying links and not sharing sensitive information online.
